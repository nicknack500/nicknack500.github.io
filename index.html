Problem statement: Parking at USC sucks.

Parking on USC's campus is a nightmare and students are unable to quickly and efficiently find parking spots on campus before their class.

Affinity diagram: Parking at USC

Our diagram lays out the problems students commonly experience at USC, then we provided solutions sorted by the type of implementation.

Team Personas: Our Users

John Smith is a Student at the University of South Carolina. He needs to be able to efficiently find parking before his classes since he does not own a University parking pass, and instead relies upon street parking. "I just can't keep wasting 10 plus minutes of my day looking for parking before each class" - John Smith.

Team Storyboards: Usage Scenarios

The displayed storyboard shows John Smith utilizing our Parking Assistant App in order to quickly find parking for his classes. The first panel shows him using the app as soon as he gets to his car, and checking available parking. He find parking near his chosen academic building, then parks his car. He makes it to class early instead of barely on time or even late as a result of being able to know exactly where available parking is located.
